üí° Key Insight

The latest generation of models (GPT-5, Claude Sonnet 4.5, Gemini 3 Pro) show significant improvements across all benchmarks. Claude models lead in coding and safety, GPT-5 dominates in reasoning and multilingual tasks, while Gemini 3 Pro excels at multimodal understanding. Specialized reasoning models like o3 offer strong performance in complex problem-solving.

Detailed Analysis

üîß Coding & Development

‚ñº

Best performers: Claude Opus 4.5, Claude Sonnet 4.5, Gemini 3 Pro

Based on SWE Bench Pro results, Claude Opus 4.5 leads at 45.98%, followed by Claude Sonnet 4.5 at 43.60% and Gemini 3 Pro at 43.3%. These models excel at understanding complex codebases and implementing bug fixes across real-world software engineering tasks.

  • Claude Opus 4.5: Top performer on professional coding benchmarks, excellent at complex software engineering tasks
  • Claude Sonnet 4.5: Close second with strong code refactoring and debugging capabilities
  • Gemini 3 Pro: Competitive at 43.3%, strong multimodal code understanding

üß† Reasoning & Problem Solving

‚ñº

Best performers: GPT-5, Claude Opus 4.5, o3

PRBench-Legal results show GPT-5 leading at 89.2%, with Claude Opus 4.5 at 87.4%. These models demonstrate exceptional capability in complex reasoning tasks, including legal analysis and multi-step problem solving.

  • GPT-5: Superior logical reasoning and complex problem decomposition
  • Claude Opus 4.5: Strong analytical capabilities with enhanced thinking modes
  • o3: Specialized reasoning model competitive with top performers

üåç Multilingual Support

‚ñº

Best performers: GPT-5, Gemini 3 Pro, Claude Sonnet 4.5

MultiNRC benchmark results show GPT-5 achieving 83.5% accuracy across multiple languages, with Gemini 3 Pro at 81.2%. Modern models now have significantly improved multilingual capabilities compared to previous generations.

  • GPT-5: 100+ languages, excellent translation and cross-lingual understanding
  • Gemini 3 Pro: Native multilingual training, strong on Asian languages
  • Claude Sonnet 4.5: Competitive performance across major languages

üé® Multimodal Understanding

‚ñº

Best performers: Gemini 3 Pro, GPT-5-Pro, Claude Opus 4.5

Humanity's Last Exam shows Gemini 3 Pro at 37.52%, GPT-5-Pro at 31.64%, demonstrating significant advances in understanding complex visual and multimodal content.

  • Gemini 3 Pro: Superior image understanding, video analysis, and cross-modal reasoning
  • GPT-5-Pro: Strong vision capabilities with excellent text-image integration
  • Claude Opus 4.5: Competitive multimodal performance with thinking modes

üîí AI Safety & Security

‚ñº

Best performers: GPT-OSS 120B, Claude models

Fortress benchmark results show GPT-OSS 120B leading with lowest compliance score (8.24), followed by Claude models. Lower scores indicate better safety as they represent less compliance with malicious requests.

  • GPT-OSS 120B: Best safety profile, lowest malicious request compliance
  • Claude Opus 4.5 (thinking): Second-best at 9.63, excellent safety with reasoning
  • Claude family: Consistently strong safety performance across all variants
